Settings

Connect your Sheet

▸ Google Apps Script Web App URL

Paste the URL you got after deploying the Apps Script (ends in /exec). This connects the app to your private Google Sheet — your data lives there, not on any server of mine.

▸ Sync

Pull the latest data from your Sheet, or push everything currently on this device up to it. Useful right after connecting a new phone or browser.

▸ About this connection

When connected, sealing a day or editing your saved entries writes directly to your Google Sheet in the background. If you're offline, changes save locally first and sync next time you have a connection. Nothing is sent anywhere except your own Sheet.
